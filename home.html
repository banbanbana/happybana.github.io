Origin Story

At the age of 2, I was typing C:\OHMYGOD to elicit a pulsating 8-bit rainbow from the CRT monitor. By the age of 10, I was beautifying chain letters on AOL and stealing Hotmail’s layout for my website devoted to my various RPG characters, drawings, and short stories. In high school, I had the most elaborate AIM profile you’ve ever seen, and stayed up for days on end crafting even more elaborate xanga layouts as the div, javascript, and CSS started to revolutionize the web. There’s literally nothing else I’ve spent as much of my life doing. From posters to databases to gigantic branded sliding doors, I can make pretty much anything appear from my Adobe CC and Notepad++.

Enough about work...

When I’m not working, my favorite things are bonfires, gardening, standup comedy (Hannibal Burress, Aziz Ansari, Maria Bamford, and Kumail Nanjiani are some of my current favorites, but you can’t deny the greats - Pryor, Murphy, Carlin, and Rivers), and all manner of games - card, video, board, I could go on. I can at times be brash and blunt, but I have an empathetic nature that makes me, like most creative types, very sensitive to different needs and points of view. My brain is ever solving problems big and small, visual and esoteric, and that is why I love what I do.
